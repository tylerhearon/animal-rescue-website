About Us

At Global Rescue, we are committed to taking care of our world and the creatures that we share it with. All animals deserve the same care and respect in the wild in order to coexist harmoniously.

Unfortunately, in many cases, humanity has not been kind to our animal counterparts. Here at Global Rescue we are committed to spreading awareness for the preservation and welfare of our enviroment, and caring for and protecting the endangered animals that need our help the most.

Sponsor an Endangered Animal

With 6 additional sanctuaries having opened since Global Rescues conception in 2003, we are proud to offer rehabilition for over 30 varieties of animals in our parks.

In every park, we also have several long-term or permanent care residents that cannot be released, and will require a level of care throughout the rest of their lives.
